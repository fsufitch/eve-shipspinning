The 90% Promise

The Ship Spinning Inc. slot machines will always strive to provide a 90% payout ratio, with a 5% margin of error. The slot machine will also have a clear and visible current payout display.

What's this all about?

In completely chance-based gambling, one of the most important statistics is the "payout ratio" or "payout percentage". This number is usually secret or obscure, since it provides a measure for the profitability of a certain game. That is, the payout ratio is the amount of money a player can expect to win back given an infinite number of games. You can read all about it here.

The reason this number is usually secret is because players, who want to win, naturally gravitate towards the games with the highest payout ratios. The payout of luck games is also strongly regulated in the real world to keep casinos honest. Since there are no such laws in Eve, gambling institutions are fleecing their players with apallingly low payouts.
