Giving Out Prizes

Hello, Agent {{=char.charname}}. To hand out a prize, follow these steps:
  1. Choose a prize to hand out from the list below, preferably the oldest (top) one.
  2. You can group prizes together for more efficient delivery to one person.
  3. Go to the market and purchase the item(s). Use the corp wallet. Any unauthorized personal withdrawals of money from the wallet will reflect poorly on you.
  4. Look in the "Deliveries" tab of the station to find the prize(s). Do not move prizes from here. They cannot be placed back, and this is the only place they can be contracted as Ship Spinning Inc.
  5. Select the relevant prizes, right click, and select Create Contract.
  6. Create a private contract to the winner on behalf of Ship Spinning Inc. Make sure you spell their name right, and account for numbers in the name. For example, "BL00D" and "BLOOD" are very different things.
  7. Contract duration is 3 days.
  8. Include the Award ID from the table in the description of the contract. Enter multiple numbers if you are grouping prizes together.
  9. Send the contract
  10. Click the "Done" link next to the prizes you just sent.
  11. Rinse and repeat. Tip: keep purchases for deliveries separate. Items in the delivery hangar stack ruthlessly, and they cannot be separated.
